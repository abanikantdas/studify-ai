# STUDIFY AI — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Overview

STUDIFY AI is an AI-powered Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to help students manage their academic life from one modern platform. It combines task management, notes, scheduling, attendance tracking, analytics, and an AI study assistant into one seamless experience.

The application should feel like a professional SaaS product rather than a college project.

---

# Vision

Build the best AI-powered student productivity platform that helps students organize, learn, and improve their academic performance.

---

# Goals

- Help students stay organized
- Improve study productivity
- Reduce missed assignments
- Provide AI-powered learning assistance
- Create a beautiful, modern user experience
- Work on desktop and mobile as a PWA

---
